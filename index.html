Welcome to Vinland Saga

Step into a world where medieval, Viking, and fantasy themes converge to create an epic survival and roleplay experience. Our server offers a rich variety of gameplay options, including farming, treasure hunting, bounty hunts, boss fights, and home decoration. Engage in an economy system where trading items is essential, and build your own thriving colony with Mine Colonies.

    Key Feature:
  • Plugins: EssentialsX, WorldEdit, mcMMO, and more to enhance your gameplay.
  • Custom Mechanics: Unique game mechanics tailored for an immersive experience.
  • Structures & Landmarks: Explore a world filled with custom structures and landmarks.
  • Events & Quests: Participate in special quests and seasonal events.
  • Custom Items & Mobs: Discover custom items and battle unique mobs.
    Server Rules:
  • No explosives in the village.
  • Engage in trading and avoid stealing.
  • Use money to buy items.
  • Claim only licensed land.
  • Buy a license to trade.
  • Purchase land to build your house.
  • Assign jobs and roles within the community.
  • Follow additional rules to ensure a fun and fair environment.
